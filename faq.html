FaQ

  • What kind of work do you specialize in?
    • My focus is on character artwork in an Anime & Kemono art style. I also specialize in original character design work.
  • How long does it take for you to complete a piece?
    • It takes me roughly about 1 week to complete a commission, depending on the complexity of the piece, this can vary.
  • What kinds of projects are you willing to take on?
    • I will generally accept any kind of request, as long as it does NOT depict any acts of severe violence, gore, pornography, or use of drugs.
  • How do you accept payment?
    • Payment is handled via paypal, you will receieve a link to fulfil payment via email if your request is accepted.
